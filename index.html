What is STEM?

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics is a umbrella term used to group together the distinct but related technical discipline of science technology engineering mathematics.



Stats

Why less people are enrolled in STEM

We believe that already this subjects are hard. So as hard as it is, it is not fun to learn. We also believe that it is easier to see how things are done than to read how things are done. In matric this subjects are failed probably because the is 80% theory and 20% practical, hence less people are enrolled to this subjects in higher education.
